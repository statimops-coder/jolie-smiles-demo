Specialized Services

We focus exclusively on implants and dentures—that's all we do. That's what makes us experts.

Dental Implants

The gold standard in tooth replacement. A titanium post is surgically placed in your jawbone, and a custom crown is attached. It's the closest solution to having your natural tooth back—and it lasts 25+ years.

Why choose implants? They preserve jawbone density, don't damage adjacent teeth, and let you eat anything without worry. You brush and floss normally. You smile with complete confidence.

Best for: One or more missing teeth where you want a permanent, natural-looking solution.

Implant-Supported Dentures

The best of both worlds: implants anchor dentures securely so they never slip or click. You get the stability of implants with the affordability of dentures—and the speed of same-day teeth.

Why choose this? If you have multiple missing teeth and want security without the cost of individual implants per tooth. Implant-supported dentures stay put and look natural.

Best for: Full mouth reconstruction where you want stability, comfort, and the ability to eat whatever you want.

All-on-4 Dental Implants

A complete smile makeover using just 4 implants per arch. This revolutionary technique gives you a full set of new teeth without needing an implant for each tooth. It's the game-changer in restorative dentistry.

How it works: Four strategically placed implants anchor a full set of custom-made teeth. Dr. Truong designs every tooth for your face, bite, and personality.

Best for: Patients who've lost all or most teeth and want a permanent, full-mouth solution in minimal time.

Same-Day Teeth

Walk in with missing teeth. Walk out with a full smile. Our in-house lab creates teeth in hours, not weeks. You don't leave with temporary teeth—you leave with permanent-looking restorations.

How it works: 3D imaging → Design → 3D printing and milling → Custom fabrication → Placement—all in one day, right here in our office.

Why it matters: No gaps. No embarrassment. No metal temp teeth. Immediate confidence.
