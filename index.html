Specialized Services

We focus exclusively on what we do best: implants and dentures.

Dental Implants

Permanent tooth replacement that looks, feels, and functions like your natural teeth. A titanium post is surgically placed in your jawbone, and a custom crown is attached. It's the closest solution to having your real tooth back.

Why implants? They preserve jawbone, don't damage adjacent teeth, and last 25+ years with proper care. You can eat anything, brush and floss normally, and smile with confidence.

🦷
Before/After: Implant Gallery

Implant-Supported Dentures

A hybrid solution: implants anchor dentures securely so they don't slip or click. You get the stability of implants with the affordability of dentures.

Best for: Multiple missing teeth where you want security and natural appearance without the cost of individual implants per tooth.
